Guess the Letter

This is a simple guess the letter game.

Game

Rules

  • The player has 10 tries to guess the letter, from A-Z, the computer has generated.
  • The player can launch the game by pressing any key.
  • If the key pressed matches the randomly generated computer leter than the player wins a point and another letter will be generated.
  • If the key pressed does not match the randomly generated computer letter, then the guess count decreases by 1 for every wrong letter guessed.
  • If the player is out of guesses their losses will increase by on and the computer will generate another letter
  • Letters already guessed will dislay on the "Guessed Letters" area of the screen.
